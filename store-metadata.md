## Description
Nudge is your personal trading companion that helps you make disciplined, rule-based decisions before, during, and after every trade.

Before you trade, Nudge walks you through a pre-trade checkpoint — verifying your setup against your own rules for risk, position sizing, and conviction. You'll get a clear verdict: Proceed, Adjust, Wait, or Stop.

Start each day with a daily plan — set your intention, check your emotional state, and define your watchlist. End each day with a structured review to rate your discipline and capture lessons learned.

Track your trades in a personal journal with emotion tagging, setup pattern analysis, and performance insights. See your streaks, win rates, and discipline scores on a clean analytics dashboard.

Key Features:
- Pre-trade checkpoint with rule-based verdicts
- Daily planning with mood and intention tracking
- End-of-day accountability reviews
- Trade journal with emotion and pattern tagging
- Analytics dashboard with performance metrics
- Streak tracking for consistent habits
- Fully offline — all data stays on your device
- Dark theme designed for focus

Nudge doesn't give financial advice or connect to brokers. It's a mindset tool — built to help you stay consistent, self-aware, and accountable in your trading journey.
